Who are we?

We are a small but efficient and experienced team, committed to highly polished games focused on key features. We try to fail fast, fix fast and take data-driven decisions.

 

Our goal when starting a new project is to have a playable build released the public as soon as possible, test the core gameplay and monetization elements and identify potential issues. We believe that determine if the product has potential in an early stage is key to save time and money.

We practice an incremental development, making it easy to understand what went wrong, learn and iterate. Every feature adds weight and reduces flight time. Keep it lightweight to maneuver.

From scratch → to final release → to liveOPS.
