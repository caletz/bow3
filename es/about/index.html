Who are we?

We are a small experienced team committed to make highly polished games focused on key features. We try to fail fast, fix fast and take data-driven decisions.

 

Our goal when starting a new project is to have a playable build released to the public as soon as possible, so we can test the core gameplay elements and identify potential issues whether is the meta-game or the monetization as soon as possible. Determining if the product has potential in an early stage is key to save time and money.

We practice an incremental development, making it easy to understand what went wrong, learning from it and iterate. Every feature adds weight and reduces flight time. Keep it lightweight to maneuver.

From scratch → to final release → to liveOPS.
